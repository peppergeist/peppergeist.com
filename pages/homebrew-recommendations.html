Homebrew Recommendations

This page contains interesting Homebrew formulae I have installed.
angband
The classic dungeon-exploration game based on The Lord of the Rings. Not much more to say.
asciiquarium
Displays an acsii aquarium animation in your terminal window. Very nice as a screensaver for monitors that are not in use.
cmatrix
Displays terminal raindrops in the style of The Matrix. Neat. Manages to segfault at random sometimes.

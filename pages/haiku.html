In my spare time, I write haiku. On this page, they are arranged by year, and then by topic.

2018

"One Year"

Ready for summer
I sit behind the window
Glaring at the sleet
Ready for winter
I sit underneath the shade
Squinting at the sun
The red leaves of fall
Trees will not show life again
Until next spring comes
The new blooms of spring
Life indefatigable
At least until next fall

"The Workday"

Waiting for the bus
I think of things I must do
My mind is spinning
While I am at work
My hands labor for their wage
My mind is elsewhere
On the bus to home
I regret how little time
The evening has left
While I am at home
My mind only thinks of work
My hands idle, I sigh

"Sleeping on The Couch"

Sleeping on the couch
Like a guest in my own home
What lousy service
This couch is comfy
Why does my couch feel better
Than the bed I built?
Because I built it?
Could the mattress be too firm?
Or is it too soft?
Maybe small changes
Are comforting, even if but
Sleeping on the couch

Overview

Since nearly all mechanics of this game are already well-documented in the wiki and other various places, this page is primarily focused on overall strategy and useful tips.

Items

There are many items in Spelunky, and they all fall under three specific categories:
  • Consumable - Multiple amounts of these items may be picked up and used one at a time. This category includes things like ropes and bombs.
  • Passive - These items, once picked up, cannot be dropped. They can be picked up multiple times, but it does not affect their efficacy.
  • Active - These items must be held in the spelunker's hands in order to be used. You may only hold one of these items at a time.

ITHOM

ITHOM is a text-based roguelike played in the terminal window. You play as a scamming miscreant who has made a living selling snake oil to people paranoid of catching an epidemic illness which leaves its victims in a permanent coma. A person becomes infected by falling asleep within the vicinity of someone who has already contracted it. Doing so imprisons that person's consciousness in a large, paradoxial dream dungeon known to its inhabitants as Mononeir. The marble halls of Mononeir wind slowly downwards into deeper and more dangerous depths. It is known there exists an archive, an academy, a prison, and more somewhere below where most inhabitants have taken refuge. Only by fighting your way downwards through the dreamlike labyrinth will you be able to find and confront the host of the dream, and perhaps find a way out...

ITHOM is currently very in progress and only has preliminary dungeon-generation algorithms and map structures currently working. The project page is private as I would like to make it public once it has more content, and a website is in development for it as well.

Voltimand Land

Voltimand Land is an homage to the Wario Land series of games, featuring a treasure-hunting protagonist looting the remains of a spooky castle. It showcases many of the iconic mechanics, such as the charge and ground-pound, and adds a couple more. The art style is strongly inspired by Locomalito's L'abbaye des Morts, a little explorative platformer, and GameBoy Color palettes.

PRONG

PRONG is a classic-style RPG also in development. All the sprites on this site are assets from PRONG. You play as a rogue mailer daemon on a quest to return a letter. On the way, you can make friends, eat food, trick-or-treat, and possibly save the world. More information coming later (by later I mean as I flesh out the story more).

About me

Howdy. I'm Colton, and this is my website. I enjoy programming, reading, making music and making software. My hobbies include ghosts, peppers, and little green ghouls. Also, I make software in my spare time. I am a backend software developer. I build software that interfaces between the accounting team and selling team at Amazon.com. My work allows people to import products which are normally not available in their country direct via Amazon. I'm currently working on automating my own job.

I studied applied mathematics at Texas A&M University with a specialization in computer science and encryption/decryption. Perhaps one day I'll be able to put that to good use.
